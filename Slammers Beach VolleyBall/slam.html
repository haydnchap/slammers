About Slammers

Slammers Beach Volleyball is a small local volleyball club house. Slammers is open to people of all ages.
We offer casual volleyball, professional competitions, social events, fitness and fun. Slammers is home to a licensed bar open everyday, happy hour is from 6 to 7pm. Slammers is also available to hire for all sorts of functions, from birthdays to weddings, you name it.
